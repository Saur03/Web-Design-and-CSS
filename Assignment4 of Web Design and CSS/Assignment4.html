Virat Kohli

One of the greatest cricket players of our time

Biography

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an Indian international cricketer. He is considered one of the best batsmen in the world.

He started his cricketing career at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks, showcasing exceptional talent and dedication to the sport.

Records

  • Fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs).
  • Named ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times.
  • Holder of numerous records in Test and T20 formats.
  • First cricketer to score 80 international centuries.
  • On 15 November 2023, Kohli scored his 50th ODI century to surpass Tendulkar's haul and became the first player to score 50 centuries in ODI cricket.

Achievements

Virat Kohli has received numerous awards and accolades throughout his career. Some of his notable achievements include:

  • Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian award.
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, India's highest sporting honor.
  • Arjuna Award for outstanding achievement in sports.
  • Named Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World multiple times.
